Our Mission

At Code Karma we believe in the democratizing influence of the world wide web. With the help of a strong web presence nonprofits can reach wider audiences, raise more funds and do more good.

However, many businesses can't afford to build awesome websites that represent their mission and accomplish their goals. This is where Code Karma comes in. Developers looking to build their resumes, learn new skills or give back can sign up for nonprofit projects and earn points by completing them or answering tech-related questions in the community feed. This way nonprofits get the help they need free of charge, and developers get to work on projects with real-world impact.
